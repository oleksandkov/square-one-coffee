## Executive Summary

**Square One Coffee (SOC)** is a family-owned specialty coffee chain headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta. Founded in May 2015 by Jonathon and Brandy Brozny, the company has grown from a single location to a vertically integrated coffee enterprise with 6 operating locations (7th coming soon), an in-house roasting operation, a subscription service, and a pizzeria.

### Key Figures
- **Founded**: May 2, 2015
- **Founders**: Jonathon and Brandy Brozny (married couple, 6 children)
- **Locations**: 6 active + 1 coming soon (Edmonton metro area)
- **Employees**: Estimated 80-120 (based on location count and hours)
- **Related Businesses**: 3 (Anecdote Coffee Roasters, Doorstep Barista, Stone & Wheel Pizzeria)

### Founding Story
> *"We were eager to start a new venture that would enable us to work alongside each other and would offer us the freedom to be with our six children as much as possible. In a way, we were leaving all we had built up before and going back to square one."*

The name "Square 1" reflects the founders' decision to start fresh—a complete reset to build something aligned with their values of family, community, and quality.

- Full-service café experience
- Extended hours for evening social occasions
- Emphasis on "home-like" atmosphere
- Community gathering space positioning

### Vertical Integration
SOC's control of the coffee supply chain from roasting to cup represents a significant competitive advantage and operational complexity:

```
Bean Sourcing → Anecdote Roasting → SOC Cafes → Customer
                        ↓
              Doorstep Barista (D2C)
                        ↓
              Wholesale (other cafes)
```
